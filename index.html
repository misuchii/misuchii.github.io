About me

Somewhere between static and silence,

a signal hums — not loud, not clear,

but persistent, like an idea you forgot to name.


The system boots, slowly.

Numbers fall like dust.

Time loops, or maybe stutters.

We build without knowing why.


In empty corners, pixels whisper.

Across invisible grids, nothing moves —

and still, it all feels intentional.


Not real, not false.

Just here. Just blinking.

A sentence without a sender,

waiting for someone to scroll past.
